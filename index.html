From folders to an organized library

Four steps from install to a library you can actually browse.

1

Point at your folders

Tell Delle Model where your media lives. Subfolders become creators automatically. No naming convention required.

2

Sync your library

Hash-based indexing finds doubles, fingerprints media, and builds a portable library — all on your disk.

3

Backfill metadata

Recover post dates, descriptions, and tags from gallery-dl + yt-dlp sidecars — even for files downloaded months ago.

4

Browse & enjoy

Open any creator's profile — videos, images, favorites, tags. Powerful viewer, swipe mode, and an activity timeline when your media has dates.

Everything you need

Built around the way creator content actually lives on disk.

A profile per creator

Every creator becomes a profile with all their videos and images in one place — tags, favorites, notes, links and feats. Subfolders auto-detect on sync, so your library organizes itself by the right person.

Hash dedup

Automatic exact (SHA-256) + perceptual (dHash) hashing on sync. Finds doubles across re-downloads, quality variants, and reposts.

Metadata backfill

Re-queries the original sources via gallery-dl + yt-dlp to recover post dates, descriptions, tags retroactively. Works even months after download.

Timeline + activity histogram

When your media has post dates, browse a creator chronologically — year-by-year histogram, drill-down to monthly, click a bar to filter the feed. No competitor has this.

Local-first & portable

Your whole library is a single portable file. No cloud, no tracking, no account. One-click export, restore on any machine — Windows / macOS / Linux.

Built for bulk

Multi-select across pages: mass-tag, mass-move, mass-recycle. Soft-delete recycle bin with one-click restore. Opt-in LAN access from your phone.

See it in action

A closer look at the features that make your library easy to live in.

Statistics

Understand your whole library at a glance

A built-in dashboard adds up everything you have — models, videos, images, favorites and playlists — plus your total video runtime. See your most-viewed videos and most-used tags side by side, so you always know what's in your library and what you actually reach for.

  • Totals across your entire collection
  • Most-viewed videos, ranked
  • Most-used tags as a live bar chart
Bonus when you have dates

Dated media? You also get a timeline.

When your media carries post dates — recovered automatically from the original sources — every creator also gets an interactive activity histogram. Spot the busy years, the quiet stretches, the comebacks. Click a year to drill into months, click a bar to filter the feed.

  • Year-by-year, drill down to monthly
  • Click a bar to filter the feed instantly
  • Built from real post dates, not file timestamps
