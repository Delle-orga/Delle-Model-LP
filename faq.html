Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Delle Model, from installation to advanced features.

General

Delle Model is a locally hosted desktop application that lets you manage, organize and sync your models and their multimedia content (images and videos). Everything stays on your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
Yes, Delle Model is completely free. You can download and use the application at no cost and with no subscription.
Absolutely. Delle Model is hosted locally — no data is sent to an external server. All your content remains private and secure on your machine.
Delle Model is currently available for Windows. macOS and Linux versions are planned in our roadmap and will be coming soon.

Features

From the main interface, click the create button. You can set a name, description, profile picture and cover photo. A local folder will be automatically created to store the associated content.
Each model is linked to a local folder. Delle Model monitors that folder and automatically syncs images and videos that are added, removed or modified. Your library always stays up to date.
Delle Model includes a built-in download system that lets you fetch images and videos from compatible sites directly into the corresponding model's folder. Simply paste the URL and start the download.
"Feats" let you link multiple models to the same video. For example, if a video features two models, you can tag them both and find that video from either model's page.
You can assign custom tags to each image or video. Then use the search filters to sort your content by tag, model, date or media type.
Yes! You can add models, videos and images to your favorites. Your favorites are accessible from a dedicated section for quick access.

Technical

No, Delle Model works entirely offline. An internet connection is only needed to download content from external sites.
Delle Model automatically generates thumbnails for each imported video. You can also customize the thumbnail by choosing a specific frame or uploading your own image.
Delle Model supports common image formats (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF) as well as standard video formats (MP4, MKV, AVI, WebM). Additional formats may be added based on demand.
